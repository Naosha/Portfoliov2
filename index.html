Who am I?

Hi, I'm Naomi!
I'm a self-taught full-stack developer based in Wells, Somerset. I'm passionate about learning all things to do with web development and making beautiful websites. Check out some of my work in the project section.
Since completing my first web dev bootcamp as a lockdown project in 2020 I've spent every spare moment studying new and exciting technologies. Honestly, I think I may have a slight addiction to Udemy courses!
I'm currently looking for job oportunities where I can contribute and continue to learn and grow. If you have a position that would be a good fit for my skills and experience then please get in touch.
I love nature, freediving, crochet and playing tenor horn in my local brass band.
